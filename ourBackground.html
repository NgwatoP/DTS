Our Background

DTS security Services is a private security company based in Bluedowns, Cape Town, Western Cape Province, and it registered with PSIRA (Private security industrial Ragulatory Authority) in 2014. It initially operates in the Western Cape region by providing Unarmed, Uniformed, security guards(all grades) for events, Mall/shopping centre activations, communities and annual cultural activities.

The company had grow organically and diversified to include all forms of security services such as Armed response and technical responses team, close protection unit(VIP protection unit) investiagtions units and CCTV and Alarm installations.
